Why we chose this project:

Tunnels yield a high-concentration of Co2, greatly polluting the surrounding resources from air to large bodies of water. In addition to the grave social consequences, mass pollution can also stunt economic growth by making an area seem unappealing and unworthy of investment.
Our design supplants filters on tunnel ceilings that converts Co2 into Oxygen, converting the problem into the cure. But we don't stop there. Atop each tunnel, we plan to build a giant 'balcony' designed for the needs of that particular area, whether communal or corporate.

Where we chose this project:

BalcoNY is an architectural public works project that will add a pedestrian park area akin to Washington square park to the west side highway.
The underpass built to accommodate its design
will be rigged with C02 scrubbers that will
purify the air and sequester carbon safely
for repurposing.
